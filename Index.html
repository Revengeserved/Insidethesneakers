Turns Out Rock Bottom Has a Basement

Inside the Sneakers is a personal website about resilience, survival, and documenting the human story behind hard times.

I’m a disabled veteran, and for six years, I’ve been fighting to get SSDI. Right now, we have no food stamps, no EBT, and every door feels closed. But this isn’t where my story ends. I want this blog to be a place of positivity—a space where I share how I stay motivated, how I support my kids, and how I find little ways to keep giving back. My hope is that, even from this rock bottom, I can offer strength, courage, and a reminder that we don’t have to give up.
