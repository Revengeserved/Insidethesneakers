Inside the Sneakers by Ronn Popagorgio
The life behind the sign

People hate two things:
what they fear
and what they envy.

And nobody’s envious of the guy holding the sign.

Inside the Sneakers is a personal project about the truth behind public struggle, the story behind the assumptions, and the real life people usually do not stop long enough to ask about.
