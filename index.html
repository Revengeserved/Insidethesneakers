The life behind the sign

People hate two things:
what they fear
and what they envy.

And nobody’s envious of the guy holding the sign.

Inside the Sneakers is where I tell the truth behind public struggle—what people assume, what they miss, and what it actually costs to keep standing when life keeps swinging.
